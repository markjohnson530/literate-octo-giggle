World Modifications:

  • The world border is set to 1 million in each direction from 0,0. This is significantly smaller than the default 30 million, but big enough to allow for impressive builds to be created that can realistically remain undiscovered for years.
  • Phantoms do not naturally spawn but spawn eggs can be purchased with in-game money at the shop.
  • Bedrock can be purchased with in-game money at the shop.

Dragon Egg Event:

  • Every Saturday at 7pm EST, a Dragon Egg is generated in a random location within 20,000 blocks of spawn in the Overworld. The coordinates are publicly released in chat and the Discord.

Currency:

    In-game currency can be obtained on the server by selling cobblestone using /sell. You cannot sell any other item.

Stone Miner Scoreboard:

    The Stone Miner scoreboard is a friendly competition seperated from money featuring the top ten players that have mined the most stone. Commands regarding this can be found in the Command section.
