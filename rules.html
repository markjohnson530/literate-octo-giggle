Server Rules

1. Hacking, cheating, or using mods that give gameplay leverage over other players are not allowed and may result in a jail or ban. Mods that are allowed include such as OptiFine / other performance improvement mods, Armor and effect status HUD mods, or purely aesthetic mods.

2. Do not spam chat or use hate speech, slurs, or other excessively offensive language.

3. Using bugs, glitches, or attempting to use unintended server mechanics is not allowed. The only exception is carpet duplicators, and Primed TnT duplication for the sake of farming. (Both must be within reason and this is not guarenteed to last.)

4. Advertising or promoting other servers/websites is not allowed.

5. Griefing and raiding are allowed on this server.

6. Do not share share personal information and/or identities of others without their explicit permission. i.e., doxxing.

7. Immoral behavior that may not be specifically mentioned in these rules can be acted upon. Moderators reserve discretion for retroactive rule enforcement; just because a rule does not exist, or a loophole does exist, does not mean you will not be banned for it.

Breaking any of these rules will be judged on a fair case-by-case basis and moderators have discretion to utilize any of the server's 3 main punishments: a ban, jail, or mute.
